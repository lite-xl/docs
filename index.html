    Lightweight and Powerful Text Editor.

    Fast and extensible. No Electron required.

    Get Started GitHub
    Lightweight

    30 MB of RAM, 5 MB of disk space. Lite XL runs on many devices without performance issues.

    Powerful

    Multi-cursor support, code completions, command palette and tab-dragging to maximize your productivity. Lite XL is intuitive and easy to learn.

    Extensible

    Written in C and Lua, Lite XL is easily extensible. Lite XL has a diverse plugin ecosystem that supercharges it into an IDE-like editing experience.

    Cross-platform

    Built on SDL, Lite XL runs on Windows, Linux and macOS. Porting to other systems is trivial.
