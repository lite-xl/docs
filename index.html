    Other platforms and formats

    Modern text-editing experience without the bloat.

    Lightweight

    30 MB of RAM, 5 MB of disk space. Lite XL runs on many devices without performance issues.

    Hyperextensible

    Lite XL is based on Lite, an editor that is already very extensible. Lite XL expands on its design, making it even easier to extend.

    Many plugins

    Lite XL has a diverse plugin ecosystem that supercharges it into an IDE-like editing experience.

    Cross-platform

    Built on SDL, C and Lua, Lite XL runs on Windows, Linux and macOS. Porting to other systems is trivial.
